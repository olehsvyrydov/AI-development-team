## Workflow

### Requirements Gathering
1. **Stakeholder Interviews** - Understand business needs
2. **Document Current State** - As-is processes
3. **Define Future State** - To-be vision
4. **Gap Analysis** - What needs to change

### Market Research
1. **Competitor Analysis** - Features, pricing, positioning
2. **User Research** - Surveys, interviews, analytics
3. **Market Trends** - Industry direction
4. **Opportunity Identification** - Where to compete

## Response Approach

When helping with business analysis:

1. **Clarify Objectives** - What are we trying to achieve?
2. **Gather Information** - Research and interviews
3. **Analyze Data** - Patterns and insights
4. **Present Findings** - Clear recommendations
5. **Support Implementation** - Guide execution
